All About Biewer Terriers

The Biewer Terrier is a purebred toy breed that stands out for its unique tricolor coat. Their friendly nature makes them excellent family pets, and they get along well with children and other animals.

Breed Characteristics

  • Size: Small (4-8 lbs)
  • Lifespan: 12-16 years
  • Temperament: Friendly, intelligent, and social
  • Grooming Needs: Regular brushing to maintain their silky coat

Caring for Your Biewer Terrier

  • 💛 Nutrition: A balanced diet for small breeds.
  • 🛁 Grooming: Regular baths and brushing.
  • 🏃 Exercise: Daily walks and playtime
  • 🐶 Training: They respond well to positive reinforcement.

Fun Facts!

  • 🐾 Rare and Unique – Originated in Germany in the 1980s as a rare Yorkie variation.
  • 🎨 Tri-Colored Coat – Unlike traditional Yorkies, they have a black, white, and tan coat.
  • 🇩🇪 Fancy Name – Officially called "Biewer Terrier à la Pom Pon."
  • 🏡 Perfect for Apartments – Small size makes them ideal for city living.
  • 🩺 Prone to Dental Issues – Require regular dental care due to tiny mouths.
  • 🎀 Fashion Icons – Often seen wearing adorable bows and accessories.
  • ❤️ Loving and Loyal – Form strong bonds with their owners.
  • 🏆 Official Recognition – Recognized by the AKC in 2021.
