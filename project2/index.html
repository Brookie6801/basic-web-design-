Welcome to Biewer Terriers!

A breed of elegance and charm, the Biewer Terrier is a delightful companion known for its tri-colored silky coat, playful personality, and affectionate nature.

Why Choose a Biewer Terrier?

  • 🐾 Loving & Loyal – These little dogs form deep bonds with their owners.
  • 🎾 Energetic & Playful – They love to play and stay active.
  • 🏡 Great for Apartments – Their small size makes them perfect for cozy homes./li>
  • 🦴 Low Shedding – Their long, silky coat is hypoallergenic with proper care./li>

About the Breed

Originating from Germany in the 1980s, the Biewer Terrier was developed from the Yorkshire Terrier. With their charming personality and adorable looks, they quickly became a beloved companion breed.
