Biewer Yorkies

Biewer Yorkies are a rare breed of terrier that originated in Germany. They have tri-colored fur that consists of black, white and brown. Biewer Yorkies make great companions as they are very playful and cuddly. Since these terriers are a rare breed, they are quite expensive but well worth the money!

Caring for Biewer Yorkies

Biewer Yorkies need are relatively easy to care for. They need a bath once a week and their fur brushed daily. In terms of food, they should have a good variety of meat. Biewer yorkies love to play with toys like tennis balls and chew toys. Biewer Yorkies also love to walk so be sure to get them a leash and harness and off you go! (Do not forget the poop bags just in case!)

Toys and Exercise for Biewer Yorkies

  • Leashes
  • Chew toys
  • Tennis Balls
